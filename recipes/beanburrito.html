Bean Burrito

Description

A staple of my childhood for when its too early for dinner, but you are hungry after school/work. Easy to make and very filling, I'm sure these will quickly become one of your favorite afternoon snacks.

Ingrediants

  • Flour tortillas
  • Refried beans
  • Shredded cheese
  • Fritos

Steps

  1. Lay tortilla out on plate
  2. Spread refried beans in center of tortilla
  3. Drizzle cheese on top
  4. Microwave on high for 45-60 seconds
  5. Top with fritos
  6. Roll it up and enjoy
