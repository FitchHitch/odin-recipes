Chili Dip

Description

This recipe is simple, quick, and delicous. It can be made in the oven or microwave, and is perfect snack for those football Sundays

Ingredients

  • 1/2 stick of cream cheese
  • Half can of chilli(I prefer no bean)
  • 1 cup shredded colby jack cheese
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Grab a 9" glass pie dish
  2. Spread cream cheese with a spoon to make a nice bottom layer
  3. Evenly distribute chilli on top of cream cheese
  4. Top with cheese
  5. Microwave for 4 minutes, or bake for 15 minutes at 350F
  6. Enjoy with your favorite tortilla chips
