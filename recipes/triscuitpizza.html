Triscuit Pizza

Description

These little guys are great for a quick bite, or for a late night snack. Small, portable, and tastey, they are sure to become a classic in your house!

Ingredients

  • Triscuit cracker
  • Bottle of pizza sauce
  • Sliced or shredded cheese of choice

Steps

  1. Place crackers on baking sheet or microwave safe plate
  2. Add a dollop of pizza sauce on top of crackers
  3. Place cheese on top of pizza sauce
  4. Bake for 5 minutes at 350F or microwave for 1 minute
